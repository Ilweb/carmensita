What is “Going tapas"

Wellcome!!!
¡¡¡Bienvenido!!!

“Going tapas” has nothing to do with the size of your dish, it has to do with what is in your dish and how you eat it.

“Going tapas” means sharing; slow food; not eating everything at the same time and having different drinks depending on what you are eating.

“Going tapas” means a unique gastronomic experience in which you are going to try much more than two courses and a couple of wines.

…And thats what we are offering, the ultimate tapas experience

Si puedes leer esto no te vamos a explicar lo que es irse de tapas. Lanzarote

Arid Lanzarote, one of the Canary islands off the coast of West Africa, is known for its year-round warm weather, beaches and volcanic landscape. East-coast resort Puerto del Carmen has whitewashed villas, a harbor and dive centers. Caletón Blanco is a string of northern coves with calm, clear water for snorkeling, and Famara beach has cliffs for hang gliding and surf schools.
